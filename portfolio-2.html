about me

A bit about
who I am

I'm an instructional designer with a passion for creating learning experiences that actually stick. I specialise in translating complex information into clear, engaging content — whether that's an interactive e-learning module, a facilitator guide, or a well-structured course.

I draw on adult learning theory, UX thinking, and a healthy obsession with clarity to design materials that meet learners where they are. I care about outcomes, not just deliverables.

Outside of work you'll find me tinkering with new tools, exploring what good design looks like, or drinking too much coffee.
